Pi Kappa Alpha is an international fraternity based around four overarching qualities: Scholarship, Leadership, Athletics, and Gentlemanly Qualities. Here at the Beta Beta chapter of Pi Kappa Alpha, we strive to excel at all four of these qualities; from winning the intramural athletic award for 19 years in a row, placing first overall in sorority hosted philanthropy events in the 2013-2014 school year, to even maintaining a high grade point average and having half of the fraternity in STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) majors.

Our brotherhood is impactful on the community, engaged in our University, caring about our fraternity, and trained how to be successful in both our education and our careers. We have a diverse environment that allows everyone to be able to enjoy a true college experience, as well as being a part of something that will last a lifetime.
